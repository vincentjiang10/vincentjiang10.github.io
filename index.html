ABOUT
Hello! I'm Vincent, an undergraduate student majoring in computer science at Cornell University. Here's more about me:
I followed my interest in computer programming and mathematics from high school into college. I found instant connection with problem-solving and the creative yet logical aspects of computer science. By building diverse projects, applications, and games, I familiarized myself with the software development and design process and was fascinated by it. I find computers magical in their ability to transform abstract thoughts and ideas into practical pieces of software. These and more have inspired me to become a software developer.

I love to learn, which has motivated my teaching endeavors: I find being a teaching assistant an exciting and rewarding experience, helping students with coding projects and assignments.
Moving forward, I am applying my enthusiasm for learning about computer science and new technologies as I begin my journey as a software engineer.
